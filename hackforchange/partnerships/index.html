Opportunities for Partnerships

Sponsorships

Opportunities include providing fund (or direct donations) for:

  • Event space
  • Food/drink
  • Swag (bags, t-shirt, stickers, etc.) for attendees
  • Prizes for top projects
  • Content experts to lead challenges or workshops (provided/developed by either Code for SF or you)

Perks include:

  • Free admission to the event
  • Logo/company will be included in our media/PR plan
  • Thanked on stage during opening party and closing reception

Community Partners

Opportunities include:

  • Collaboratively develop a challenge/project (with project lead from your organization)
  • Recruit project leads for existing challenges
  • Recruit volunteers for event support
  • Identify civic user testers
  • Spread the word about the event

Perks include:

  • Free admission to the event
  • ‘Thank you’ announced during opening party and closing reception
  • Logo will be listed under ‘Community Partners'

Program Partners

Opportunities include:

  • Bring and facilitate activities, workshops, and/or performances for event
  • Recruit volunteers for event support
  • Spread the word about the event

Perks include:

  • Free admission to the event
  • ‘Thank you’ announced during opening party and closing reception
  • Logo will be listed under ‘Program Partners'
