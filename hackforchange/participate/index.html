How can I participate?

There are many ways to participate in NDoCH SF weekend. This amazing weekend will feature opportunities to collaborate on projects in a welcoming environment. Projects are open source and we learn together so we can contribute to the civic and social life here in San Francisco. Don't know how to code? That's great! We need people with all sorts of ideas, insights and experiences.

The following are some ways you can plug in to this event.

Propose a project

Got a great idea? Or even some code already that you want help on? Great! Take a look at the project guidelines, register for NDoCH SF, and submit your project

Collaborate on an existing project or challenge

Check back here as projects and challenges get added. You'll be able to reach out and join up with projects looking for collaborators. In the mean time, you can register for the event. You'll get updates on new projects and opportunities to connect with collaborators between now and the event weekend.

Volunteer for the weekend

Don't want to hack, but want to contribute to making the weekend successful? We're looking for volunteers to help make NDoCH successful. You can sign up to be a volunteer below. We'll get in touch shortly.

Not sure yet? Register and make your mind up later.

Maybe you're just curious? Just want to check this thing out without committing too much? You can still register and show up that weekend. If you plan on floating or checking things out, or want to join a team later, we'll get you all that important information between now and the event.
