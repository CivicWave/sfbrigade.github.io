Privacy Policy

We use Google Analytics, a web analysis service provided by Google to track and analyze usage patterns on www.codeforsanfrancisco.org. This information is collected in an anonymous fashion by installing a cookie (small file) on your computer and sent directly to Google. We do not share this data with any other organizations.

Google's privacy policy has more information about how they store and use this data.

Google's opt out page has links to a browser addon you can install to opt out of being tracked.

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
